## How to know it's working
- **Search Console → Links** (left menu) will start listing external sites pointing to you.
- **Search Console → Performance → Queries**: watch your average **Position** drop (improve)
  for `mujtaba khan ios developer`, `mujtaba khan upgrad`, `mujtaba khan flutter`.
- Re-check `site:mujtabakhan.com` in Google after ~1 week — your pages should appear.

## What NOT to do
- ❌ Don't buy backlinks or use "submit to 500 directories" services — Google penalizes these.
- ❌ Don't repeat the same anchor text in spammy comments.
- ❌ Don't expect to rank for the bare word "Mujtaba" or "Mujtaba developer" — too crowded.
      Realistic wins: **your full name + niche** (`Mujtaba Khan iOS developer`, `Mujtaba Khan upGrad`).
